About

Leopoldo Commisso was born in Carmen de Areco, a town in the Buenos Aires Province of Argentina.

His aspirations and thirst for music lead him across the Americas from USA to Bolivia. Each living experience provided leopoldo with a rich and multi-cultured mindset which is reflected with in every music he composes.

Leo currently resides in Penedo, Rio de Janeiro, Brasil.
